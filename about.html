About Somali Health Review Platform

Our mission is to provide transparent, verified, and actionable hospital evaluations across Somalia, empowering citizens and improving healthcare services nationwide.

Our Purpose

Somali Health Review is designed to make hospital evaluation transparent and accessible. By allowing verified citizens to submit evaluations, we ensure the data is trustworthy and actionable for administrators and policymakers.

  • Empowering citizens to provide real feedback
  • Increasing hospital accountability and transparency
  • Using data to improve patient care and hospital services
  • Helping identify systemic strengths and weaknesses

Platform Impact

Since launching, the Somali Health Review has helped citizens provide transparent feedback to hospitals across Somalia. This has led to:

  • Increased hospital accountability
  • Better resource allocation based on patient feedback
  • Enhanced patient satisfaction and healthcare quality
  • Data-driven decision making for administrators
