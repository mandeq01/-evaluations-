About Somali Health Review Platform

This platform was designed and developed by Your Name Here to provide transparent, verified, and actionable hospital evaluations across Somalia. Its goal is to empower citizens and improve healthcare services nationwide.

Home Evaluate About Contact Login

Platform Purpose

Somali Health Review allows citizens to submit honest evaluations of hospitals, helping administrators understand strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities to improve patient care.

  • Empowering citizens to provide real feedback
  • Increasing hospital accountability
  • Improving patient satisfaction and healthcare quality
  • Supporting data-driven decision making
